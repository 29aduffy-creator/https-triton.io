Heritage & Vision

Born of
Ancient Waters

TRITON draws its essence from the god of the sea — sovereign, immovable, eternal. Each timepiece carries the trident as more than a mark; it is a covenant between the maker and the deep, a promise of precision that holds under every pressure the world can exert.

Founded in Geneva's watch-making quarter, TRITON unites centuries of Swiss craft with a design language that refuses the ordinary. The dial is an ocean at midnight. The case, an anchor against time itself.

The Movement

Crafted at
Every Scale

The calibre inside each TRITON is decorated by hand, finished to haute horology standards, and regulated across five positions. We do not rush the sea.

01

Swiss Lever Escapement

A traditional lever geometry, refined with modern micro-tolerances for unmatched shock resistance and energy efficiency under deep pressure.

02

Côtes de Genève Finishing

Every rotor, plate, and bridge is hand-decorated with parallel stripes visible only under magnification — beauty for its own sake.

03

Trident Signed Movement

The gold trident motif is engraved on the rotor of every piece. The signature of the sea, hidden beneath the dial, worn against the wrist.

04

Anti-Magnetic Architecture

Silicon hairspring and non-ferrous alloy bridges ensure accuracy in fields up to 15,000 A/m — from boardroom to ocean floor.
